Superlinked-Entre Case Study

Technical case study demonstrating Superlinked's work for prospective client communications. Author, PM.⧉

Task

Superlinked's CEO Daniel Svanova and COO Ben Gutkovich gave me some background context material, a cursory brief, and asked me to write a case study of their VectorOps for improving feed personalization outcomes for Entre, a tech professional networking platform.

Process

After reoutlining the brief, some background study, and setting some early check-in milestones, I asked for a point person to fill in the blanks of the Superlinked vectorization process and the specific details of Superlinked's process with Entre.
Over the next two weeks, Superlinked's Tech Lead Balázs Kemenes and I collaborated iteratively, not only on articulating the details of Superlinked's work with Entre but also opened up discussions with the CEO and COO on both strategy and future general development and documentation directions.

Results

Technical marketing document.
Social Platform Presentation (Entre Case Study)⧉
Ongoing collaboration with Superlinked on VectorHub.
"Superlinked is a VC-funded machine learning infrastructure startup with a very complex and technical product. Robert took a week to thoroughly understand the software developers who use our product and how the product works and then wrote our first proper technical case study. We have been using the case study ever since to explain to everybody what we do. We are going to be working on multiple pieces with Robert soon - if you are a technical founder or an engineer trying to get your ideas across, Robert is your guy!"
