24px Rocks + Punk Rocks

Historically informed, whimsical storefront copy for NFT artwork, 24pxRocks. Author.

Task

"Write a narrative about our NFT artworks. Be creative. Briefly discuss our motivation as creators in the NFT space. Have fun with it. Differentiate it from its closest reference NFT products."

Process

I adopted a fun, poetic tone to capture the whimsy of the NFT creators, playing on the history and culture of punk rock. I quickly researched the NFT space, its history, some of its key players, as well as 24pxRocks and PunkRcks to understand their relationship to their closest NFT references.

Results

24px Rocks started selling at 0.03 eth and sold out at 0.1 eth. 84 of 100 24px Rocks sold out in the first 30 hours.
